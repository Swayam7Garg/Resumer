How the flow works

On successful signup we simply redirect you to login – FastAPI should handle user persistence and JWT generation at login.

  • Form validation on name, email, password, and role.
  • On success, we redirect to the login screen.
  • All API traffic uses /auth/signup at your FastAPI base.

Update the FastAPI endpoint paths in script.js if your backend uses different routes.
